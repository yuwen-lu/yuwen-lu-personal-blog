My second user test.

2019-05-21 usability PV:

Several days ago I had my second user test on a EEG Brain Activity project.

I have to say I like this test more than the first one on augmented reality rubics cube instruction, mostly because of two reasons: firstly it doesn’t have so many mysterious bugs, and secondly the test is quite simple. Basically the test are divided into two parts, both requiring us to put on a EEG brain activity detecting device. The first task asks us to take a look at a series of pictures and tell the tester if we liked it or not, and the second one asking us to take a look at a series of websites and tell if we like its design or not.

The test is meant to collect our brain activities, learn about how the patterns in these activities connect to our emotions and attitudes. The interesting thing I didn’t expect is that I pretty like the websites showed to us during the test, which the tester simply grabbed from the website Webflow. The designs of those websites are either clean or fancy, which attracted my eyes at first glance.

Seeing all these cool website designs, I cannot help but to think about my desire to learn web develpment for all these months: I have been wanting to learn for long, but I have been just lazy. When can I seriously start to learn something on my own?
