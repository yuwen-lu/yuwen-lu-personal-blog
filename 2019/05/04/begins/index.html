And the story begins...

2019-05-04

Servus!

Traditional stories always begin with expressions like “Many years ago…”, but mine doesn’t. I live in the moment.

I am Yuwen Lu, a Bachelor student in Software Engineering from China, and currently I am exchanging in Technische Universität München in Bavaria, Germany. As a student in technology (even though I don’t want to fall myself into any category), I am particularly interested in Human Computer Interaction (HCI), Education Technology and Usability Engineering.

I use this site to live my life and share my learning experience. You can play around the site, click any button as you like, and if you are lucky enough to find any problems, please write to me at this address. I really appreciate your help to improve this site!

Come and follow along with me into the rabbit hole, if you would like to ;)
